# Lumina - Design & Feature Requirements

## 1. Design Philosophy & Identity
*   **Vibe:** Ethereal, minimalist, premium, and calming.
*   **Colors:** Material You base.
    *   Primary: Deep Midnight Blue (contrast) / Soft Cream (background).
    *   Secondary: Warm Gold / Muted Copper (accents).
    *   Backgrounds: Subtle, slow-moving aurora gradients.
*   **Typography:**
    *   Quote Body: Elegant Serif (e.g., Playfair Display or Merriweather).
    *   UI/Authors: Geometric Sans-Serif (e.g., Montserrat or Roboto Flex).
*   **Iconography:** Thin-line, elegant icons.

## 2. User Flow
1.  **Onboarding:** Rapid taste establishment and notification permissions.
2.  **Home (Daily View):** Focus on today's quote.
3.  **Preferences:** Categories and delivery timing.
4.  **Widget Studio:** Creative hub for home screen customization.

## 3. Screen Detailed Design

### Screen 1: Home Screen (The Daily Spark)
*   **Layout:** Edge-to-edge immersive view.
*   **Top Bar:** Minimal. Floating Menu (left) and Saved/Bookmark (right) icons.
*   **Center Stage:**
    *   Large, centered quote in Serif font.
    *   Smaller Author name in Sans-Serif below.
*   **Bottom Actions:** Floating "Share" (center) and "Like/Heart" (right).
*   **Interactions:**
    *   Fade-in reveal animation.
    *   Long press to copy text.
    *   Pull-to-refresh with haptic feedback.

### Screen 2: Preferences (Curating the Light)
*   **Delivery Scheduling:** Modern clock-face time picker.
*   **Content Curation:** Selectable chips/visual cards for categories (Stoicism, Motivation, etc.).
*   **Tone Selector:** Slider from "Direct & Punchy" to "Poetic & Long."
*   **Widget CTA:** Prominent banner linking to Widget Studio.

### Screen 3: Widget Studio (The MVP)
*   **Split View:**
    *   **Top:** Live preview on a mock Android home screen.
    *   **Bottom:** Customization controls (Tabs).
*   **Customization Options:**
    *   **Style:** Rounded corners, Background (Solid/Aurora Gradient/Transparency).
    *   **Typography:** Font Family toggle (Serif/Sans), Size slider, Color Adaptive toggle.
    *   **Elements:** Show Author, Show Logo.
*   **Interaction:** "Save Widget Style" with celebratory animation.

## 4. Notifications
*   **Rich Notifications:** Show full quote and "Share" action directly in the shade.
*   **Tone:** Engaging and inspiring copy (e.g., "The darkest nights produce the brightest stars...").

## 5. Unique Selling Points (USPs)
*   **Widget-First Mentality:** Primary value delivered through the home screen widget.
*   **Immersive Calm:** Zero clutter, no ads, high focus.
*   **Real-time Widget Preview:** Interactive and accurate styling tool.
